Short Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning (TMLR) Group in the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, advised by Dr. Bo Han.

My research interests lie in the broad areas of robust and reliable machine learning, including OOD Detection, adversarial robustness, and label noise robustness.
