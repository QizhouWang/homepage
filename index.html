Short Bio

I achieved my PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, advised by Prof. Bo Han. I will soon join the Imperfect Information Learning Team at RIKEN AIP as a postdoctoral researcher, led by Prof. Masashi Sugiyama. I have worked as a visiting research student at RIKEN AIP, collaborating with Prof. Masashi Sugiyama and Dr. Gang Niu, and at Cornell University, collaborated with Prof. Kilian Q. Weinberger. I work closely with Dr. Gang Niu, Prof. Tongliang Liu, Prof. Feng Liu, and Dr. Jianing Zhu.
